SmokePing

Port 9030

📊 What it does: High-precision latency and jitter visualization.

🎯 Why you need it: Identifying physical line issues (Packet Loss) vs. noise (Jitter).

💡 How to use it: Look for "smoke" (gray blur) around the lines to diagnose unstable streams.
